About Dr. Anugonda Srinivasa Chari

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Anugonda Srinivasa Chari is a highly respected specialist dedicated to patient well-being.

Dr. Chari is committed to delivering exceptional medical care with a focus on personalized treatment plans and advanced diagnostic techniques. My mission is to ensure every patient receives the highest standard of care in a comfortable and supportive environment.

Education

My Areas of Expertise

Comprehensive General Medicine

Providing holistic primary healthcare services for individuals of all ages. From routine check-ups to managing acute illnesses, I ensure thorough diagnosis and effective treatment plans.

  • Routine physical examinations
  • Diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses
  • Immunizations and vaccinations
  • Health screenings

Proactive Preventive Healthcare

Focusing on preventing diseases before they occur through personalized health strategies, lifestyle guidance, and regular screenings.

  • Weight management programs
  • Nutritional counseling
  • Stress management techniques
  • Early detection screenings

Effective Chronic Disease Management

Long-term care and management for chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma, aiming to improve quality of life and prevent complications.

  • Diabetes management
  • Hypertension control
  • Asthma and respiratory care
  • Arthritis and pain management
